Welcome to our website

Having grown-up in a rather frugal household that seemingly had everything it needed it was always difficult for myself to buy gifts for my close family and even now it's a struggle for my wife and kids to find something to buy for me on my birthday and for Christmas.

Improving the quality of life of your friends and family is just priceless and that is why I started this website. I just really like helping others and having others recommend useful things to me too!

Over the last decade or so I have received a lot of excellent quality of life products and tools which help me out on the odd occasion and it always brings a smile to my face when I use something that was bought to make my life easier!
